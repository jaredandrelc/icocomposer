Icon Design Guide

Creating a great Windows icon requires attention to detail across multiple sizes. Here is what to look out for:

Square Canvas

Use a 1:1 aspect ratio (e.g. 1024x1024).

Transparency

Keep the background transparent.

Silhouette

Use strong shapes that work at small sizes.

Contrast

High contrast ensures visibility on all backgrounds.

Safe Area

Leave a 10% margin around the edges.

Avoid Text

Text is unreadable at 16px. Use logos instead.
